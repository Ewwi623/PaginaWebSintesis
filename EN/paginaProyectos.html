Our Projects and Lines of Action

At Vidas Con Futuro, we understand that to fight inequality effectively we must act on different fronts. It is not enough to offer temporary relief; our ultimate goal is to provide the necessary tools for communities to break the cycle of poverty definitively. Therefore, we divide our activity into four fundamental pillars.


  • Solidarity Campaigns: We organize food, clothing, and school supply drives throughout the year. We ensure that basic needs for shelter, nutrition, and education are met in the most vulnerable sectors, coordinating direct distribution networks in community centers and low-income schools.

  • Educational Days: We conduct workshops and talks on solidarity, poverty, and human rights. We firmly believe that education is the engine of change; therefore, we raise awareness in civil society and empower youth and adults through educational programs that promote empathy, gender equality, and the defense of fundamental rights.

  • Development Projects: We promote sustainable projects so that communities can grow independently. This includes creating basic infrastructure, local entrepreneurship workshops, advanced agricultural training, and microcredit programs that enable families to generate their own income and lead their own progress.

  • Emergency Relief: We mobilize immediate support teams in humanitarian crisis situations. Whether facing natural disasters, conflicts, or unforeseen health crises, our global network activates rapid response protocols to supply clean water, emergency medical care, temporary shelter, and food to affected populations.

Each of these projects is possible thanks to the joint effort of our volunteers on the ground and the unconditional support of people like you. No real change happens overnight, but with perseverance and commitment, we are transforming statistics into real stories of overcoming adversity.

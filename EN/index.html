The Reality of Poverty

Poverty is not just a lack of income, but a multidimensional vulnerability that affects access to healthcare, education, and decent housing. Understanding its causes is the first step toward building bridges of opportunity and transforming the future of those who need it most.

To talk about poverty is to recognize one of the deepest and most persistent crises in our society. It is often reduced to an economic figure, but the reality is much more complex: it is an intergenerational cycle that limits human potential and deprives communities of their most fundamental rights.

This issue manifests itself in the lack of access to basic services such as clean water, proper nutrition, and a quality education that allows the development of skills for the future. Without these tools, people find themselves trapped in a daily struggle for survival, where long-term planning becomes an unattainable luxury.

At Vidas Con Futuro, we believe that poverty is not a permanent condition, but a circumstance that we can transform together. By addressing not only the symptoms but also the structural causes, we can create an environment where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, contribute their talent to society, and live with the dignity they deserve.


  • Global Impact: According to the UNDP, it is estimated that more than 1.1 billion people live in acute poverty, subsisting on less than 2.15 USD (1.85 EUR) a day.

  • Child Vulnerability: Almost half of the people living in poverty worldwide are children, which compromises the development of future generations.

  • Education Gap: 60% of young people in vulnerable households fail to complete secondary education, drastically limiting their future job opportunities.

  • Access to Healthcare: The lack of financial resources reduces life expectancy by up to 15 years compared to high-income sectors.

These data are not just numbers. They represent lives, paused dreams, and families waiting for an opportunity. At Vidas Con Futuro, we work to change these statistics through direct intervention programs and community support.


At Vidas Con Futuro, we commit to doing everything possible to help the most vulnerable families so they have an opportunity to have the life they truly deserve.

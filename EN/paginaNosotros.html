Our History and Commitment

At the end of 1975, Pedro Negro was on a business trip in Africa when he witnessed, in a small village, the deep economic inequality and malnutrition compared to the most developed regions of the world. Impacted by this reality, he decided to make a radical change in his life: in 1976 he left his old job and, using his own savings, founded Vidas Con Futuro, dedicating himself completely to the mission of helping others.

Although the project initially received little support, the landscape changed in early 1980 thanks to the Speedwagon Foundation. This organization took a strong interest in the NGO and began to support it with large, constant donations and advertising campaigns. Thanks to this crucial boost, social interest grew exponentially, attracting a large number of collaborators and volunteers.

Today, Vidas Con Futuro is a Spanish NGO that has spent half a century transforming realities thanks to the generosity of our donors. Currently, we are present in more than 60 countries, with various volunteers at each center dedicated to protecting the families and people who need it most. Thanks to our rapid expansion and global reach, we continue to grow to ensure that help always reaches where it is most needed.
